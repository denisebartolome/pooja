work

Based in New York City, you can usually find me at the Stand Up NY, the New York Comedy Club, the Stand, the Greenwich Comedy Club and the Grisly Pear. I like to tell stories about culture, race, womanhood and singledom. As a comedian I want you to laugh at my expense and also get a better understanding of the google headlines that most of you just scroll over!

story

Pooja Mallipamula is a white-washed Indian chick who moved to NYC in pursuit of comedy. She has a degree in Engineering and Journalism, neither of which she puts to actual use in the real world. She is a Business Analyst during the day and a Stand Up Comic at night. She is really a gay boy trapped in a woman's body. She is already prepared for her future husband to come out of the closet after having children together. She is still innocent enough to believe that voting in elections will actually make an impact in our status quo. She is also a social butterfly obsessed with fashion, chocolate and over analyzing everything. Most important of all: she is the Brunch Queen!
